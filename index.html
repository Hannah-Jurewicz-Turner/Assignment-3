“We do lots of stuff. The only way you are going to have success is to have lots of failures first.” - Sergey Brin


Sergey Brin co-created Google, the biggest search engine in the world. Google allows people to pull out their small electronic devices and have all the information known to man in their pockets. Google allows us to get all the newest information on news from anywhere in the world, the latest updates for our favourite tv shows and we’re able to educate ourselves and grow our knowledge. Google has truly transformed our society.

Sergey Brin was born August 21, 1973 in Moscow, Russia. His family moved to America in 1979. Brin graduated from the University of Maryland with a degree in computer science and mathematics and then entered Stanford University where he met Larry Page. While in university, Brin and Page started working on a new search technology. He got his masters degree in 1995 from Stanford.

A few years later, Brin and Page raised about 1 million dollars from investors and family to continue their search engine project. They renamed their project “Google” which they got from the term “googol” which is the number one with 100 zeroes afterwards. They finally made their project into a cooperation and Brin became the president of technology of the company. Between 1998 and 2004, their success has risen immensely and by 2004, the website was used 200 million times per day. Also in 2004, the corporation issued its public offering which made Brin over 3.8 billion dollars. In the same year, Brin became the youngest person to appear on the list of Forbes 400 richest people in the world. Google has been on the rise ever since. In 2011, Brin gave up his duties and instead became the director of special projects.Now, he has two kids with Anne Wojcicki although they split up in 2015.

He launched a google glass which is a new way to share and get information quickly. He is also working on self driving cars and has been since 2009 but is only starting to seriously make progress more recently. In May 2021, he was the 9th richest person in the world with a net worth of around 100 billion dollars. Brin is very invested in a lot of charities and has recently donated 100 million dollars to a parkinson’s research group since his mother has the disease and he has a 50% chance of developing it as well. Brin has a lot of his money invested in several businesses but one business he co founded was 23andme. He helped create this company with his ex wife. 23andme is a genetic test that can tell you a lot about your dna through a test tube of spit. This company is worth around 4 billion dollars. He is now supposedly married and is president of Alphabet.
